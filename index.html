About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Calgary. I have a passion for research and solving real-world problems through statistical methods and machine learning.

Research Interests

Functional Data Analysis

Statistical methods for analyzing data that vary over a continuum.

Neural Networks

Deep learning architectures and their theoretical foundations.

Machine Learning

Developing and applying ML methods to complex problems.

Network/Graph Analysis

Statistical approaches to understanding network structures.

Bayesian Methods

Probabilistic approaches to statistical inference.

Interactive Tools

Building interactive statistical visualization tools.
